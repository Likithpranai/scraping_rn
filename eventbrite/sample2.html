About this event

Silk & Smoke: Allure Chet Baker Night

Chet Baker’s music drips with silky elegance and smoky allure. His haunting trumpet, intimate voice, and turbulent charm immortalized him as jazz’s timeless romantic, forever painting nocturnal melodies of love and longing.

絲絨與煙霧：Chet Baker 的迷人之夜

Chet Baker 的音樂散發著絲絨般的優雅與煙霧般的魅力。他令人心碎的小號、親密的歌聲與動蕩的魅力，將他永遠定格為爵士界永恆的浪漫詩人，描繪愛與渴望的夜晚旋律。



Immerse yourself in a special tribute to the legendary jazz trumpeter Chet Baker. Featuring the talented Kowloon Swing Outfit, the evening promises a heartfelt reinterpretation of Baker's soulful melodies and intimate style, which have left an indelible mark on jazz.

誠邀您參與向傳奇爵士樂小號手 Chet Baker 致敬的特別活動。當晚將由出色的九龍搖擺樂隊（Kowloon Swing Outfit）帶來對 Baker 那充滿靈魂的旋律和親密風格的深情演繹，這些都是他在爵士樂界留下的不朽印記。

Kowloon Swing Outfit

Led by David Batchelor, the Kowloon Swing Outfit spans a diverse range of jazz styles from traditional to modal jazz of the late 1960s. The band has attracted large audiences on multiple occasions and frequently collaborates with swing dancers to provide a lively and accessible music experience.

由 David Batchelor 領銜，Kowloon Swing Outfit 橫跨從傳統到1960年代末期的模態爵士的多元爵士樂風格。這支樂隊曾在多個場合吸引眾多觀眾，並經常與搖擺舞者合作，帶來充滿活力且易於接近的音樂體驗。


Explore the Artistry of Musicians


David Batchelor

David Batchelor, with 30 years of international experience performing in Europe and Asia, has a rich and diverse musical style that spans from classical to rock, and of course, jazz. His major influences include jazz greats like Chet Baker, Miles Davis, Louis Armstrong, and Clark Terry.

David Batchelor 擁有30年的國際經驗，曾在歐洲和亞洲演出，他的音樂風格豐富多元，從古典到搖滾，當然還有爵士樂。他的主要影響包括爵士樂巨匠如 Chet Baker、Miles Davis、Louis Armstrong 和 Clark Terry。


Toshi Horiguchi

A Tokyo native, Toshi's musical journey was ignited during his teenage years when he discovered the enchanting sounds of soul music and jazz, particularly influenced by the captivating styles of Marvin Gaye and Bill Evans.

Based in Hong Kong, Toshi adopts an adventurous approach to his crati, fearlessly delving into a wide range of musical genres that have influenced his artistic sensibilities. Through collaborations with local and international musicians, he blends diverse musical influences, resulting in performances that transcend boundaries and captivate audiences. As he continues to navigate and contribute to the dynamic music scene in Hong Kong, Toshi remains commited to exploring new horizons and establishing himself as a versatile and dynamic force within the everevolving tapestry of the city's musical landscape.

來自東京的Toshi在青少年時期發現了靈魂音樂和爵士的迷人聲音，特別受到Marvin Gaye和Bill Evans的風格影響啟發。目前居住在香港的Toshi採取大膽的音樂探索方式，無畏地深入多種音樂類型，這些都深刻影響了他的藝術感性。通過與本地和國際音樂家的合作，他融合了多元的音樂影響，創造出超越界限並吸引觀眾的表演。隨著他繼續在香港動態的音樂場景中前行和貢獻，Toshi致力於探索新的視野，力求在這個不斷演變的音樂織錦中成為一名多才多藝且充滿活力的重要人物。


Jimmy Pitman

Jimmy Pitman, originally from Chicago, Illinois, is a bassist and educator currently based in Hong Kong. Since arriving in HK in 2012, he has been gigging consistently on both bass guitar and upright bass. He has gained a reputtion as a first call player for his fluency in numerous styles and tasteful accompaniment.

Jimmy has performed recently at the 2023 Make Music Festival, 2021 Hong Kong West Kowloon Jazz Festival, the 2019 Hangzhou “Jazz On The Lake” International Jazz Festival, the 2019 “Art Of Crati” crati beer festival at the HK Asia Society, and the 2018 HK International Jazz Festival.

原籍美國伊利諾伊州芝加哥的Jimmy Pitman，是一位低音大提琴兼音樂教育家，目前定居於香港。自2012年來到香港以來，他一直積極參與電貝斯和低音大提琴的演出，以其流暢的多種音樂風格和高雅的伴奏技巧而被認為是首屈一指的演奏者。Jimmy近期的演出包括2023年的香港音樂節、2021年香港西九龍爵士音樂節、2019年杭州“湖畔爵士”國際爵士音樂節、2019年香港亞洲社會“藝術與手工啤酒”節，以及2018年香港國際爵士音樂節。


CARLOS VIERA

Carlos Vieira's drumming is the rhythmic heartbeat of any musical group, effortlessly adapting from jazz to funk to pop. Originating from Lisbon, Portugal, Carlos has studied an eclectic mix of musical styles with legendary musicians in global music capitals. His career has taken him around the world, performing with pioneering artists from Portugal and beyond, and sharing his knowledge at the Hot Club Jazz School of Lisbon. His global footprint includes performances in numerous countries and residencies at luxury venues like the Mandarin Oriental in Hong Kong and The Ritz Carlton in Shanghai. Carlos's beats are a testament to his passion and versatility, laying down the rhythm for audiences worldwide.

卡洛斯·維埃拉的鼓點是任何樂隊的節奏心臟，他能毫不費力地從爵士樂轉換到放克樂再到流行樂。他來自葡萄牙里斯本，曾在全球音樂之都與傳奇音樂家學習多種混合音樂風格。他的職業生涯讓他周遊世界，與來自葡萄牙及其他地方的開創性藝術家合作表演，並在里斯本的熱門爵士俱樂部爵士學校分享他的知識。他的全球足跡包括在多個國家的表演和在如香港文華東方酒店和上海麗思卡爾頓酒店等豪華場所的駐場表演。卡洛斯的節奏是對他的熱情和多才多藝的證明，為全世界的觀眾奠定節奏。


This event serves as a dynamic hub for emerging and experienced artists to converge, explore, and refine their artistic talents. It's an opportunity for meaningful connections and creative exchanges within our community. Whether you are an integral part of the jazz community or newly exploring its rich history, join us in a unique setting that fosters the evolution of music and nurtures a community shaping the future of the arts.

這個活動是新興和經驗豐富的藝術家聚集、探索和提升藝術才能的活躍中心。它是在我們私密社群內建立意義連接和創意交流的機會。無論您是爵士樂社群的一份子，還是剛開始探索其豐富的歷史，歡迎參加我們獨特的活動，共同促進音樂的演進，塑造藝術社群。


Allure Friends Privacy Policy
Allure Music Salon is committed to protecting your privacy and complies with the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance. This policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, sharing, and protection of personal data.

Types of Personal Data Collected

- name, email and phone number

Personal data is used to send you messages. If you choose not to receive such communications, an opt-out option is available.

Personal data is treated confidentially, disclosed only to authorized personnel or as required by law. We will use a third-party email system to send you emails.

Personal data is retained only as long as necessary for the purpose for which it was collected and is protected using adequate security measures to prevent unauthorized access or loss.

Access to and Correction of Personal Data

You have the right to access and correct your personal data held by Allure Music Salon. Requests can be made to Allure Music Salon via email at alluremusicsalon@gmail.com or through other provided contact methods.


承諾保護隱私

Allure Music Salon 致力於保護您的隱私，並遵守《個人資料（私隱）條例》。本政策概述了我們關於收集、使用、分享及保護個人資料的做法。

收集的個人資料類型

姓名、電郵及電話號碼

個人資料用於向您發送資訊。如果您選擇不接收此類通訊，可以選擇退出。

個人資料會保密處理，僅向授權人員披露或根據法律要求披露。我們會使用第三方電郵系統向您發送電郵。

個人資料僅在收集目的所需的時間內保留，並採用適當的安全措施防止未經授權的存取或丟失。

存取及更正個人資料

您有權存取和更正Allure Music Salon 持有的您的個人資料。可以通過電郵至 alluremusicsalon@gmail.com 或其他提供的聯繫方式，提出請求。

Allure Music Salon: Evenings of Music and Mastery

Allure Music Salon, nestled in Tai Hang's historic Century Stone House, Hong Kong, hosts enthralling musical evenings and workshops. Our Incubating series and Masters events spotlight emerging talents and seasoned maestros, fostering connections and inspiring audiences. Join us for a transformative experience where creativity shapes the future of music.

Allure Music Salon 位於香港大坑歷史悠久的世紀石屋，舉辦迷人的音樂之夜和工作坊。我們的孵化系列和大師活動突顯新興才能與資深大師，促進互動並啟發觀眾。加入我們，體驗在創意塑造音樂未來的轉變性體驗。
